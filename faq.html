Frequently Asked Questions

Questions:

What is the best time to visit Rizal?

The best time is during the dry season (December to May) for better hiking and outdoor views.

Are there entrance fees for tourist spots?

Yes, most spots like Treasure Mountain and Pinto Art Museum have minimal entrance fees ranging from PHP 100 to PHP 250.
